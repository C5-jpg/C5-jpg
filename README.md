I'm an undergraduate at **Shanghai Jiao Tong University**, passionate about the intersection of **Computer Science** and **Social Science**. My research interests lie in:

- 🕸️ **Graph Neural Networks (GNN)** — modeling relational & networked data

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🔬 GraphNet — Multi-Agent Social Simulation
> A MARL framework exploring **emergent social behaviors** in networked agent populations. Built on top of PyTorch and NetworkX, it simulates cooperation, competition, and information diffusion across graph-structured environments.

**Stack:** `Python` `PyTorch` `NetworkX` `Gymnasium`

---

### 🤖 LeRobot — Robot Learning with Foundation Models
> Contributing to [LeRobot](https://github.com/huggingface/lerobot) — an open-source framework by Hugging Face for training robot manipulation policies with imitation learning and reinforcement learning.

**Stack:** `Python` `PyTorch` `Isaac Sim` `ROS2`

---

### 📖 ChineseBabyLM — First Steps in Language
> A **Chinese language acquisition** study inspired by the BabyLM Challenge. Training small-scale language models on child-directed speech data to understand how linguistic structure emerges from limited input.

**Stack:** `Python` `Transformers` `Tokenizers`

---

### 🏆 MCM/ICM — Mathematical Modeling
> Meritorious Winner in the **Mathematical Contest in Modeling (MCM)**. Applied optimization, statistical modeling, and simulation techniques to solve a real-world complex systems problem.

**Stack:** `MATLAB` `Python` `LaTeX`
